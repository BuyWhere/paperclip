## The Problem with Precision

Most astrology platforms demand your exact birth time. They say it's "more accurate." But this creates three barriers:

### 1. You might not know it

Many people don't have access to birth certificates with precise times. Some were born before records were digital. Some simply weren't told.

### 2. You might not want to share it

Birth time + location is biometrically identifying. It can be used for identity verification, stalking, or other purposes you didn't consent to.

### 3. It overwhelms before it helps

A full chart with 12 houses and 20 aspects is data paralysis. You get stuck in analysis before you take action.

## Our Philosophy: Start With What You Know

Your sun sign captures your **core identity** — your vitality, your ego, your life force. It's accurate enough to be meaningful. It's accessible enough to be shareable. It doesn't require sensitive data.

Then we layer BaZi — which *does* use your birth date (not time) for Year, Month, and Day pillars. The Day Pillar (your core self) is the most important anyway.
