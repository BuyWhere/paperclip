## The Premise

Success isn't random. It's patterned. And those patterns show up in the archetypes.

*Aries, Leo, Sagittarius + Fire/Wood Daymasters*

## The Pattern

Success isn't about *which* sign or *which* element — it's about **alignment**:

1. **Self-awareness** — Knowing your archetype and how you actually work
2. **Strategy alignment** — Pursuing goals in ways that match your nature
3. **Timing alignment** — Acting when cosmic weather supports your archetype
4. **Happiness alignment** — Defining success in terms that actually satisfy you
